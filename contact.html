Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can you come to my location?

For routine service, we typically schedule within 24-48 hours. For urgent repairs, we can often arrive within 2-4 hours. Emergency services are available 24/7 with response times of 30-60 minutes depending on location.

What areas do you service?

We service the entire Atlanta metropolitan area including Marietta, Decatur, Alpharetta, Roswell, Sandy Springs, Brookhaven, Dunwoody, Smyrna, and surrounding communities. Call us to confirm service in your specific area.

Do you charge extra for coming to my location?

No! There's no additional charge for our mobile service within our standard service area. The convenience of having us come to you is included in our competitive pricing.

What types of repairs can you do on-site?

We can perform most common repairs including oil changes, brake service, battery replacement, alternator/starter repair, basic engine diagnostics, transmission service, tire installation, and many other services. If a repair requires specialized shop equipment, we'll let you know upfront.

How do I pay for services?

We accept cash, check, and all major credit cards. Payment is due upon completion of service. We provide detailed invoices for all work performed.

Do you guarantee your work?

Yes! All of our work comes with a guarantee. Parts are covered by manufacturer warranty, and our labor is guaranteed for 30 days or 1,000 miles, whichever comes first. We stand behind our quality workmanship.
