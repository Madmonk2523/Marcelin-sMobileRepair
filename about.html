About Marcelin's Mobile Repair

Your trusted mobile auto repair experts in Atlanta

Meet Thesky Marcelin

My name is Thesky Marcelin, proud owner of Marcelin's Auto Repair. Serving Atlanta since 2021, my passion for cars started as a kid, watching my dad turn wrenches and bring engines back to life. That same passion drives me today—keeping you and your car moving forward.

Growing up in a family where automotive repair was more than just a job—it was a craft—I learned early that fixing cars isn't just about replacing parts. It's about understanding the relationship between driver and machine, and ensuring that connection remains strong and reliable.

After years of working in traditional auto shops, I realized that many people faced the same challenges: finding time to drop off their car, arranging alternative transportation, and dealing with the inconvenience of traditional repair shops. That's when I decided to bring the shop to the customer.
